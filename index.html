About Game

Echo of the Abyss is a psychological horror
platformer that takes you on a haunting
journey through the fragmented corridors of
your own mind.

This psychological horror platformer blends tense
exploration, intricate environmental puzzles, and a
deeply unsettling atmosphere where the very world
shifts and distorts in response to your diminishing
sanity. You awaken as a nameless, shrouded figure in
a crumbling, dreamlike asylum with no memory of
your past. Your only goal is to descend through the
layered landscapes of your consciousness, each level
a manifestation of different psychological states
—from the oppressive, institutional halls of denial to the
chaotic, nightmarish zones where specific phobias
and traumas have taken physical form.

Features

Dynamic
System

The world literally changes based on your mental state. As
your sanity deteriorates, the environment transforms
around you - walls shift, paths rearrange, and reality
becomes unstable.

Fear

Shadows" hunt by sound and movement patterns, "Trauma
Echoes" repeat painful moments from your past, and
"Phobia Entities" represent specific deep-seated fears.

Adaptive
Soundscape

The audio design dynamically shifts with your psychological
state. At high sanity, you hear subtle environmental cues
and distant memories.

newer)

Processor: Intel Core i5-4460 or AMD
FX-8350

Memory: 8 GB RAM

Graphics: NVIDIA GeForce GTX 960
4GB or AMD Radeon R9 380 4GB

DirectX: Version 12

Storage: 25 GB available space (SSD
recommended)

Sound Card: DirectX compatible

internet connection for activation
